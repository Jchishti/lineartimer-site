Linear Timer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Overview

Linear Timer is a productivity timer app. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data — including your custom timer intervals, presets, schedules, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage (SharedPreferences / UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

Linear Timer does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage in any way.

Third-Party Services

Linear Timer does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

Notifications

If you enable scheduled timers, the app uses local notifications (processed entirely on your device) to remind you when a scheduled timer is about to start. No notification data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

Linear Timer does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with the new date.
